About me

I'm Scott. I've been designing and helping teams build apps and online services for almost 15 years now.

I know that when teams are empowered to improve the lives of customers they've gotten to know first-hand, with solutions they've devised and developed themselves, incredible things happen.

Motivation, creativity and team effectiveness skyrocket. Along with the success of their product and business.

Too often though, the opportunity to leverage our teams' talents is utterly squandered, thanks to an inability to work in ways that build trust, and ways of working that rewards what's easy to measure, rather than what matters.

If you and your team(s) would like to explore and adopt new ways of working that work for you, your customers and your business based on proven principles I'd love to hear from you.
