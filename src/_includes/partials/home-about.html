About me

I'm Scott. I've been designing and helping teams build apps and online services for almost 15 years now.

When it comes to design problems, the knottier and nerdier the better. Which is probably why I've spent much of my career in finance, designing complex services and data-rich apps that help professionals make better decisions faster.

I'm a huge advocate of user-centric design and agile development practices, with a track record in successfully leading teams that deliver the right solutions, in the right way, sooner.

I do this by calling upon many years of experience, designing and testing all kinds of interfaces, and an intensely collaborative approach to discovery and design. This is because I know that when everyone understands our users, everyone can be empowered to make better decisions, which lead to better results faster.
