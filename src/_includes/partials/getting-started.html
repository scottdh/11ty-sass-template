Getting started. How it works

  1. Free introductory chat, to discuss challenges and goals

    Typically lasts 30 mins. If I think I can help, and you like what you hear... we move on.

  2. I meet the team(s) I’m to work with.

    I spend up to a day meeting team members 1:1. Their buy in is essential and it helps me understand how I can best help.

  3. I put together a tailored programme that covers the first 1 - 3 months

    I’ll provide a basic outline of proposed activities and outcomes. If you and the team agree - we sign contracts.

  4. The team(s) and I get cracking, with regular reviews

    I work with the team. We review progress against outcomes fortnightly. A rolling monthly contract means you’re in control.

  5. Achieve the performance your teams are capable of, and your business needs

    You bask in the glory of the incredible work your teams are doing.
