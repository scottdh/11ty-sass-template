What we’ll work on and how.

I’ll work with you and your product teams on a regular basis via a mix of workshops and coaching sessions, tweaking, nudging and examining everyday practices for continuous improvement. All while working on your business’ most pressing challenges!

Strategy and focus

Move forward faster, together.

I’ll help you create a strong, shared understanding of exactly what your product does now, and is envisioned to do later, getting everyone on the same page. We’ll do this by mapping your product in a way everyone can understand, gathering insights and candidly assessing product performance to identify the biggest value next steps. By prioritising tasks and outcomes, not solutions and outputs, your teams can work smarter and more autonomously, while collectively moving towards something increasingly spectacular.

Continuous discovery & design

Build better products that drive growth.

I’ll work with teams to build a rapid, repeatable process of product discovery and learning, to continually uncover customer needs and valuable opportunities, while rooting out assumptions and blindspots that help us minimise risks and avoid costly missteps. This same process will also enable us to test our ideas, to get more right more often, maximising impact on business goals. Done right, it’s perhaps the most effective way to improve product value, quality and long term innovation.
